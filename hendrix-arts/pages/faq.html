The #ARTISNOTARISTOCRATIC campaign is a bold initiative by HendrixArts — led by Joshua O. Makinde — to make original, commissioned pen artwork accessible to everyday people across Nigeria. Instead of charging gallery-level prices, the campaign offers three tiers (Muse, Art Collector, and Patron) that bring hand-drawn portraits to a much wider audience. It's a movement, not just a service.
Joshua is a 400-level Civil Engineering student at the University of Ibadan — Nigeria's premier institution — and the creative force behind HendrixArts. He is the national winner of the BIC Art Master Africa Competition, making him Nigeria's top entry on a continental stage. He specialises in pen drawings and portrait commissions that combine technical precision with raw artistic passion.
HendrixArts specialises in pen drawings and portrait commissions — hand-drawn on high-quality paper using pen. Every piece is original, crafted by hand, not printed or digitally produced. Subjects can include individuals, couples, families, or custom compositions.
Muse (Free): Competition slot — enter for a chance to win a free commissioned portrait. No payment required.

Art Collector (₦7,000–₦10,000): A fully commissioned portrait, A4 or A3 size. Includes frame options and delivery.

Patron (₦15,000–₦20,000): Premium large-format A2 portrait. Ideal for multiple subjects, complex scenes, or gifts. Comes framed and signed by the artist.
While completing your booking form and payment, Upload your reference photo on the Form. Please upload a clear, well-lit photo where the subject's face and features are visible. The higher the quality of the reference photo, the better the portrait outcome.

Typical turnaround is 7–14 business days depending on the tier and complexity of the piece. Patron Tier commissions may take up to 21 days. We will communicate your expected completion date when we confirm your booking.
When you book, you pay 60% of the total price upfront as a deposit. This secures your slot and allows Joshua to begin work on your piece. The remaining 40% is paid upon completion, before delivery. This system protects both artist and client.
We accept only Bank Transfers. All transactions are in Nigerian Naira (₦) and are fully secure.
Yes. A receipt is automatically sent to your email after every successful payment.
Yes! We deliver to all states across Nigeria. Delivery costs may vary by location and are calculated at the point of sending out, Clients will be informed before dispatch as they will bear the shipping costs. For Ibadan and surrounding areas, same-state delivery options may be available. International shipping is not currently available but may be added in future campaign cycles.
All artworks are carefully packed in protective sleeves and rigid cardboard packaging to prevent bending or damage during transit. Framed pieces receive additional padding and corner protectors. We take great care to ensure your portrait arrives in perfect condition.
The 60% upfront deposit is non-refundable once Joshua begins work on your piece, as it covers time and materials. If you need to cancel before work begins, please contact us within 24 hours of booking. We will assess each case individually. Once the artwork is complete, the remaining 40% must be paid before delivery.
Given the hand-drawn nature of the work, extensive revisions are not possible once the piece is complete. However, we encourage you to be as detailed as possible in your brief. If there is a clear error or significant deviation from your approved reference, we will work with you to find a fair resolution.
Upon full payment, you own the physical artwork. HendrixArts retains the right to photograph and share images of completed commissions on social media and portfolio platforms for promotional purposes, unless you specifically request otherwise at the time of booking.
